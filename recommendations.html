Picks

Books and cinema that earned a permanent place in my head.

Welcome to my personal archives — a space where I treat leisure time with the same intensity most people reserve for their careers. The books and films here are not chosen because they carry a bestseller sticker or sit atop an IMDB chart. They are works that stayed with me long after the final chapter or closing credits. If a story made me think, feel, or look at the world differently, it earned its place here.

This is a living list. My curiosity never clocks out, so check back often to see what has been added to the vault.
